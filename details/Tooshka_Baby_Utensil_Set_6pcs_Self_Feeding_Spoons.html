Introducing the Tooshka Baby Utensil Set

Designed with love and care, the Tooshka Baby Utensil Set is the perfect companion for your little one's first feeding adventures. These six-piece utensils are thoughtfully crafted to make mealtime both enjoyable and safe for babies starting at 6 months of age.

Easy Grasping for Baby-Led Weaning

Each spoon features a short, rounded handle that's gentle on your baby's tiny hands, making self-feeding simple and intuitive. Ideal for baby-led weaning, they support your child's natural exploration of food and independence during meals.

Built for Safety and Comfort

Manufactured from safe, non-toxic silicone, these utensils are free from harmful substances like BPA, lead, and phthalates, ensuring worry-free use. The anti-choke barrier design adds an extra layer of protection, making them a trusted choice for parents.

Sensory Development at Its Best

The sensory bumps on the back of each utensil encourage teething and sensory play, supporting your baby's growth in a fun and interactive way. These features make the Tooshka set not just a tool, but a part of your baby's early learning journey.

Durable and Practical Design

Whether you're at home or on the go, the Tooshka set is built to last and easy to maintain. These utensils are dishwasher safe and can be boiled for sterilization, making clean-up a breeze and ensuring they're always hygienic for your baby's use.

The Tooshka Baby Utensil Set is the ideal choice for parents seeking a safe, durable, and versatile solution for their baby's feeding needs. With a focus on quality and child development, it brings convenience and peace of mind to every mealtime.
